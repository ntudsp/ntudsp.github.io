Research themes

Our work connects physical acoustics, statistical signal processing and AI methods to build practical audio systems for real-world environments.

Active noise control

Adaptive control, multichannel secondary-path modelling, causality/coherence analysis, practical ANC windows, headrests, ducts and portable noise-control platforms.

ANCFxLMSmultichannel

AI for sound

Sound event detection and localization, acoustic scene analysis, source separation, weakly supervised learning and robust machine listening for urban and indoor sound.

SELDSEDdeep learning

Soundscape and intervention

Human-centred soundscape assessment, augmentation, acoustic comfort, affective modelling and soundscape-informed control strategies for built environments.

soundscapeperceptionaugmentation

Spatial audio

Spatial capture, sound-field representation, HRTF modelling, virtual/augmented acoustic environments and spatial auditory intelligence.
